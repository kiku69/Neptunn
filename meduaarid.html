The Meduars

Location

A distant star cluster where Neptunn orbits its sun in a dim zone. The planet is covered with deep oceans and crystal mountains, the sky constantly glowing blue-green.

Environment

The atmosphere is dense and full of mineral-rich mist that affects genetics and has fostered the development of their tentacles. Neptunn's surface is full of bioluminescent plants and giant mushroom forests.

Civilization

The Meduars live in giant glass and crystal-like cities that float above the oceans. They use their intellect to create technology that merges with biological life – their machines look like living organisms.

Culture

Knowledge and intellect are their highest values. They gather cosmic information, storing it in giant "mind archives" built using black hole energy.

Military Force

Although they are not a large race in numbers, their immense intelligence and technological superiority make them a feared force in the universe.

The Elite

Leader

  • Smart, charismatic. Her word is law. Can read her enemies like they're an open book.
  • Special abilities: Telepathy. Can bind all the minds in her team into one for better tactics.

Kael Voruun

Warrior and Bodyguard

  • Loyal, quiet. Always ready to sacrifice himself for his team.

Nyx Arelith

Strategist
