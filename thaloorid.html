The Theloarians

Homeland of the Theloarians

Appearance: Majestic beings with centaur-like bodies, their skin in earth tones – sandy, moss green, bronze, or stone gray. Their eyes resemble liquid silver, reflecting the surrounding world. Along their spine run bioluminescent scales that glow like burning embers. Their giant feathered wings – in shades of golden brown, beige, and bronze – bind them to the force between sky and earth.

Main Ability: The Theloarians control the connection between earth and energy. They can direct the flow of stones, minerals, and primal forces, shaping shields, weapons, or healing streams from light and soil. They are simultaneously warriors and guardians of nature.

Origin

The Theloarians were born on planet Astra – a world where a giant Life Crystal pulsed in the heart of the mountains. This crystal gave life to the planet, kept the forests glowing, rivers flowing, and the sky clear. Astra was not just a planet – it was a living being whose heart beat to the rhythm of the crystal.

When the Meduars stole the Life Crystal, Astra began to die: forests withered, energy streams dried up, and the bioluminescence of the Theloarian people dimmed.

Motivation

The Theloarians believe that every world is sacred and its soul is worth protecting. Now their mission is clear – to journey to the Meduars' world and bring back Astra's heart, or their entire race will perish along with the planet.

The Champions

Eryon

Guard

  • Idealistic, yet young and inexperienced. In their heart burns a hope that the elders no longer carry.
  • She was born before the Life Crystal was stolen, so she carries the light of Astra within her soul. That makes her a key element of the revival of the Thelorian's world.

Kaelthor

Warrior

  • Unwavering strength and bravery. His heart burns with the want for revenge against the Meduars. Carries the pain of his people and hates weakness.
  • Weapon: Forged from the heart of Astra. Can break stone and shadow.

Iorath

Shaman

  • A deep thinker, mysterious. Hears the voice of Astra in the dying silence. She speaks in riddles that lead to the truth.
  • Abilities: Sees energy links between the earth and sky. Can travel open and travel between cosmic portals.

Lyrri-Ja

Healer

  • Compassionate and caring. Smart and calm. Gives hope to the people even in dire moments.
  • Abilities: Can make shields from light and Astra's soil to protect their minds from attacks by the Meduars.
